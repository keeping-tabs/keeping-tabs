Welcome to Keeping Tabs!

Keeping Tabs is a Chrome Extension that keeps track of idle tabs, closing them when you haven't touched them for a prolonged period of time. If you've ever had an issue with minding your tabs, then this is the extension for you! Keeping Tabs will close tabs based on specific settings you control. Download the extension in the Chrome Store today (or tomorrow because it's not there yet)!

Keeping Tabs is maintained by keeping-tabs.
